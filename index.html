Joyce Luis

Gender Economics Researcher

Joyce is an energetic and joyful researcher-consultant specialising in the field of gender economics.

Joyce has travelled across several countries of the world, investigating the socio-economic working conditions of the people who produce one of the planet's most vital sources of food - rice.

You can see Joyce's cheerful enthusiasm for her field of expertise as she poses and smiles next to the humble rice plant.

As a scientist at heart, Joyce possesses a variety of skills including:

🔍 Consultancy
📋 Project management
📈 Analytical skills - statistics, econometrics
🤝 Positivity - cheerful collaboration

When Joyce is not in her office or out in the field talking to rice farmers, she can be found tending to her many beautiful plants in her garden, playing a game of badminton, or dancing the night away.

Here, Joyce invites you to learn more about her - her work, experience, motivations, efforts, and hopes for a more equitable and sustainable future.

Feel free to explore her website further.

Salamat and mabuhay!!
  – Joyce 💚
