Research

Joyce is interested in studying the socio-economic impact of gender issues in South East and South Asian rice farming contexts.

Joyce's work has lead her to visit some of the world's most famous and picturesque rice fields in...

Her home, the Philippines;

Thailand;

And India, to name a few.

At the heart of Joyce's research, she asks the following question:

"How does gender analysis..."

"Affect the livelihood..."

"Of female and male farmers?"

An example of Joyce's research process is as follows:

Firstly, Joyce sets up the necessary equipment for data collection...

Then, Joyce conducts an interview with the rice farmers...

Finally, Joyce presents a summary of her insights.
